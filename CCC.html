About

This club mainly serves civil engineering students at Nile university. It includes different activities that every student can participate in such as academics, internships, technical skills, fun activities, and events.

Activities

Engineer Mohamed Ossama Visit

Within the framework of Nile University's interest in reducing the gap between academic education and the requirements of the labor market, today CCC hosted the engineer Mohamed Osama, the General Manager of Elfet Group for General Contracting, which established 53 projects in a period not exceeding four years. Eng. Osama is also a lecturer in Structural Design at Udemy.
